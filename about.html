About Velvet Ember

Our Story

Welcome to Velvet Ember, a place where culinary artistry meets a luxurious dining experience. Established in 2014, we have been serving our customers with passion and dedication. Over the years, Velvet Ember has grown into a renowned name with five branches across Europe, each offering the finest dining experience with unique ambiance and diverse menus.

From Italy to Germany, our restaurants are known for delivering exceptional quality and mouthwatering dishes that bring people together. Join us and become part of our journey to continue making every meal memorable!
